This GitHub repository hosts the AI solutions developed by our community of builders to address real-world health and social challenges identified with our regional partners during the startAD AI for Good Sandbox. All code is published under the MIT license, so anyone can access, learn from, and replicate it. Bridging academic innovation with on-the-ground implementation, this collection aims to empower organizations across the UAE, KSA, and beyond through responsible, scalable AI.

Developed with support from Google.org, the startAD AI for Good initiative is designed to accelerate the adoption of AI-driven solutions that address critical challenges in health and well-being across the UAE and Saudi Arabia. Learn more about the initiative here.
